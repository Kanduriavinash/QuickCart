Customer Service & Support

Return & Refund Policy

  • Return Window: Items can be returned within 30 days of delivery for a refund, exchange, or store credit.
  • Condition: Products must be unused, in original packaging, and with all tags attached. Certain items (final sale, hygiene, personalized) may not be returnable.
  • How to Start a Return: Email our support team or use the returns portal in your account. We’ll guide you through the process.
  • Shipping Costs: We offer free returns on most items. Some exclusions may apply; see product page for details.
  • Processing Time: Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive your return.
  • Exceptions: Damaged or incorrect items? Contact us within 48 hours of delivery for priority support.

FAQs & Self-Service

  • How do I track my order? Log in and go to “My Orders” for real-time tracking and updates.
  • How do I cancel or change my order? Contact us as soon as possible. Orders can be changed or cancelled before shipping.
  • What payment methods do you accept? We accept credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, wallets, and COD.
  • How do I update my address? Go to “My Account” > “Address Book” to edit or add addresses.
  • How do I contact support? Use live chat, email, phone, or WhatsApp as listed above.
